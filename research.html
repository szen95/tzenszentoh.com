Current research

My work as an undergraduate researcher and medical data scientist seeks to improve the identification of the right drugs for the right patient.

Comparisons of drugs have predominantly focused on identifying how individuals respond similarly, and there is a lack of data-driven insight into subpopulations of individuals with differential drug response.

We applied a novel machine learning algorithm to compare drug response between cancer drug pairs. This data-driven approach paves a new way for patient stratification to identify novel cancer subpopulations, their genetic biomarkers, and effective drug combinations.
